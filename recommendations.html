✨ Skincare Recommendations

  • Gentle Cleanser: Removes dirt, oil, and makeup without stripping the skin.
  • Hydrating Toner: Helps refresh, balance, and prepare skin for the next steps.
  • Vitamin C Serum: Brightens skin, helps fade dark spots, and promotes an even skin tone.
  • Niacinamide Serum: Helps reduce the appearance of pores, redness, and excess oil.
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Provides deep hydration and helps skin appear plump and healthy.
  • Moisturizer: Locks in hydration and strengthens the skin barrier.
  • Sunscreen SPF 30+: Protects skin from UV damage and helps prevent premature aging.

💖 Recommendations by Skin Concern

  • Acne & Breakouts: Salicylic Acid, Niacinamide, Tea Tree Oil, and Zinc.
  • Dark Spots & Hyperpigmentation: Vitamin C, Alpha Arbutin, Niacinamide, and Sunscreen.
  • Dry Skin: Hyaluronic Acid, Ceramides, Glycerin, and Shea Butter.
  • Dull Skin: Gentle exfoliation, Vitamin C, and brightening serums.
  • Fine Lines & Wrinkles: Retinol, Peptides, Hyaluronic Acid, and SPF.
  • Sensitive Skin: Fragrance-free products, Ceramides, Aloe Vera, and Centella Asiatica.

🧖 Weekly Treatments

  • Exfoliate 1–2 times per week.
  • Apply a hydrating face mask for extra moisture.
  • Use a clay mask if you have oily or acne-prone skin.
  • Apply overnight treatments to support skin renewal.

🧴 Body Cream Recommendations

  • Deep Moisture Body Cream: Rich creams with shea butter, cocoa butter, and nourishing oils for very dry skin.
  • Shea Butter: Helps soften skin, reduce dryness, calm irritation, and improve the appearance of rough patches.
  • Cocoa Butter: Rich in Vitamin E and helps improve moisture, skin elasticity, and the appearance of scars and stretch marks.
  • Natural Oils: Provide deep hydration, protect the skin barrier, improve elasticity, and soothe irritation.
